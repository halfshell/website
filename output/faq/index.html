What is Halfshell?

Halfshell is a graphical terminal framework that blends web-based view components with the power of command-driven workflows.

What platforms does it run on?

Halfshell will be available for Linux, Windows, and Apple OS X.

When will the first prerelease builds be available?

Roughly six months after the project meets its funding goals.

How will Halfshell be licensed?

The Halfshell framework and official packages will be open-source software licensed under the MIT license.
